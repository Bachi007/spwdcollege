Bachelor of Science

About Department

The BSC Department was established in the year 1999 with an intake of 60 students and this was increased to 90 students in the 2007 and increased to 120 students in the 2012. The department has an eminent faculty and well supported infrastructure and laboratories. The faculty keeps abreast with the latest advances in technology and ensures that hardware equipment and related software are upgraded to ensure its students are able to keep pace with current trends in technology and the industry.

Vision & Mission

Vision:

To help in making the institute in providing competitive engineering education to the learner and bring out quality professionals in the field of Bachelor of Science, who can meet the industrial needs by taking up existing, new engineering and social challenges.

Mission:

To provide quality and effective training program in the domain of Bachelor of Science through curriculum, state of art laboratories, industrial collaborative programs, effective learning and teaching process.
